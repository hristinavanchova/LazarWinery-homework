Only the finest grapes deserve to become exceptional wine.

Lazar winery produces various styles of wines, from sparkling to still and dessert wines from different grape varieties from indigenous Temjanika, Grasevina, Belan, Kratosija, Vranec to international famous grapes as Chardonnay, Sauvignon Blanc, Pinot Gris, Gewürztraminer, Pinot Noir, Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlo and Shiraz.

Lazar winery has operation with more than 30 hectares out which 6 hectares are owned by the winery and the rest are owned by 15 partners-farmers traditionally cooperating with the winery as the corporate policy is to create long-term relations for sustainable production on the best vineyard sites in Tikves Wine region.
