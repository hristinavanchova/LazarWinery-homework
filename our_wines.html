The vineyards

We have been working together with our partners on selection of the best vineyard areas and growing the best grape varieties with the sustainable grape quality that will support the grape supply for best wine production. All the vineyards are within the Tikves wine region and close distance to the winery to provide the best quality grapes and keeping the best terroir for different grape varieties.

The vineyards are planted on the rolling hills in the region with altitude of 150 to 600 m on the well drained soils rich in sandy-stone structures and clay particles helping to retain the humidity in the soil for a long period. The soil benefits the wines with ideal ripeness of the grapes, softness of the tannins, balanced acidity and pleasant minerality of the taste, creating the superb age worthy wines.
